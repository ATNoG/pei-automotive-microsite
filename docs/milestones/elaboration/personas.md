## João Silva (Daily Driver)

<img src={joao} alt="João Silva" width={220} className="persona-img" />

**Name:** João Silva
**Occupation:** Mechanical Engineer
**Commute:** Aveiro ↔ Viseu (daily, via A25)
**Description:**
An experienced driver who values safety and efficiency. Interested in automotive technology, but prefers simple, non-intrusive solutions that enhance driving without distractions.

---

### **Motivations**
- Avoid risky situations on the road.
- Use technology that provides useful information without distraction.
- Maintain a smooth, safe driving experience.

---

### **Pain Points**
- Frustrated by unexpected delays caused by accidents or roadworks.
- Distrusts apps that display outdated or unreliable data.
- Finds many alert systems confusing or poorly timed.

---

### **Goals**
- Avoid traffic jams and delays.
- Receive alerts about accidents, construction, or hazardous conditions.
- Reach destinations safely and efficiently.

---

### **Use Cases**
- Receive **real-time alerts** about accidents and roadworks.
- View **adverse weather conditions** (fog, rain, wind).
- Get **audible alerts** for speeding or unsafe driving conditions.
- Access **alternative routes** that are safer or less congested.

## Filipe Martins (Beginner Driver)

<img src={filipe} alt="Filipe Martins" width={220} className="persona-img" />

**Name:** Filipe Martins
**Occupation:** Student at the University of Aveiro
**Driving Experience:** Recently licensed driver
**Typical Route:** Occasionally drives between Aveiro and Viseu on weekends to visit family.
**Description:**
A young and inexperienced driver who is still building confidence behind the wheel. He values simple, intuitive technology that helps him anticipate risks and feel more secure during trips.

---

### **Motivations**
- Build confidence in driving.
- Feel safer through supportive technology that anticipates hazards.
- Use tools that offer clear and straightforward feedback.

---

### **Pain Points**
- Gets nervous in unexpected situations (e.g., fog, heavy rain).
- Finds complex apps overwhelming or confusing.
- Lacks trust in systems that display too much information at once.

---

### **Goals**
- Increase safety during driving.
- Receive simple and immediate alerts about risky conditions.
- Gradually improve his ability to anticipate road hazards.

---

### **Use Cases**
- Receive **audible and visual alerts** for nearby hazards.
- View **safe driving recommendations** in real time.
- Get **assistance with specific maneuvers** when needed.
