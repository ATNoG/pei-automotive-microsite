# Milestone Description

## Context

As our system grows towards a production-ready prototype, it is essential to ensure that it not only functions correctly but also complies with legal standards and is resilient against potential risks. This milestone focuses on the **Legal Requirements, Terms of Service, Privacy Policies, and Risks Assessment**.

## Goals

The main objectives of this milestone are:
1. **Define comprehensive Terms of Use** that clearly outline the system's limitations and user responsibilities while driving.
2. **Establish a GDPR-compliant Privacy Policy** to ensure user data (like location and telemetry) is handled and protected properly.
3. **Conduct a thorough Risk Assessment** evaluating both technical vulnerabilities and business/project risks.
4. **Identify necessary mitigations** to address the identified risks.

## Documents made:

1. **Terms of Use & Privacy Policy**: Document outlining the legal terms for using our app, including safety warnings, licensing, and liability limitations, as well as a privacy policy that specifies data collection, processing, and user rights under GDPR.
2. **Risks Assessment**: A comprehensive analysis of potential technical and business risks, along with proposed mitigation strategies to ensure the app's reliability, user trust, and project success.
